Cyberpunk

Add to wish list

Description:

Cyberpunk is a tactical fiction slashing game, involving two teams with 5 players in each team. Every game has 25 rounds and the team thats wins 13 of them wins the game.
Every player can choose their legend before the game start. Each legend has different abilitties.

Recommended Requirements:

OS: 64-bit Windows 7
Processor: Intel Core i5
Memory: 8GB RAM
Graphic card: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970
Online Connection: Broadband Connection
Hard-drive space: 22GB
